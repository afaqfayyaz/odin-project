Wrap Recipe

Description

This delicious homemade wrap is packed with fresh vegetables, tender chicken, and a creamy dressing, making it a perfect lunch or snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 large tortilla wraps
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or ranch dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Warm the tortilla wraps in a skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable.
  2. Spread mayonnaise or ranch dressing evenly over each tortilla.
  3. Layer the shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, shredded chicken, and cheddar cheese on the tortillas.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Carefully roll up the tortillas, tucking in the sides as you go to form a wrap.
  6. Slice the wraps in half for easier serving, if desired, and enjoy immediately.
