Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is easy to prepare and bursting with flavor. With a perfectly crisp crust, gooey cheese, and fresh toppings, it's a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water. Let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. Add the flour, sugar, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough into a circle or rectangle and place it on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  7. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top, followed by your favorite toppings like pepperoni and olives.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve hot.
