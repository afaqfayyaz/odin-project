Burger Recipe

Description

This classic homemade burger recipe is easy to make, delicious, and perfect for any occasion! Juicy beef patties are grilled to perfection and served with fresh toppings in a soft bun.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 4 slices of onion
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (optional)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Mix gently until well combined.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until cooked to your desired doneness.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to allow it to melt.
  5. Lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or skillet.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the bottom half of the buns. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of condiments.
  7. Cover with the top half of the bun and serve immediately.
